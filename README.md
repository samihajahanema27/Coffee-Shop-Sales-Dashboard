# Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## 📌 Overview
This project presents an interactive **Coffee Shop Sales Dashboard** built using Microsoft Excel. It transforms raw transactional data into meaningful insights through data cleaning, visualization, and analysis.

The dashboard helps understand business performance, customer behavior, and product trends in a simple and visually appealing way.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze monthly revenue trends  
- Identify top-performing products  
- Understand customer purchasing behavior  
- Explore sales patterns by time and category  
- Build an interactive and user-friendly dashboard  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Techniques
- Microsoft Excel  
- Pivot Tables  
- Data Cleaning & Preprocessing  
- Data Visualization (Charts & Graphs)  
- Slicers for interactivity  

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- 📈 **Total Revenue by Month** – Tracks business growth over time  
- 📅 **Transactions by Day of Week** – Identifies weekly patterns  
- ⏰ **Transactions by Hour of Day** – Highlights peak business hours  
- 🛍 **Sales by Product Category** – Compares category performance  
- 🏆 **Top 15 Products** – Based on transactions and revenue  
- 🎛 **Location Filter** – Analyze performance across different store locations  

---

## 🔍 Key Insights
- Revenue shows a steady growth trend from January to June  
- Morning hours have the highest number of transactions (peak sales time)  
- Coffee is the most popular product category, followed by tea and bakery  
- A small number of products generate a large portion of total revenue  
- Weekday sales are generally higher than weekend sales
